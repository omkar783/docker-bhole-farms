TechNova Team — Briefing

What we are, and what we will do

What TechNova Is (Current State)

  • A small software development team building websites, mobile apps, cloud infrastructure, and custom solutions
  • Target clients: startups, established businesses, and students (final-year projects)
  • Differentiators: small team = accountability; quality-first engineering; transparent communication; end-to-end delivery (discovery → support)
  • Tech stack: Next.js, React, Flutter, Node.js, Docker, AWS/Azure — modern and scalable
  • Process: 7-step pipeline — Discovery → Planning → Design → Development → Testing → Deployment → Support

What We Will Do (Team Strategy & Action Plan)

"We build quality software, end-to-end, for businesses and students — and we win because we're small, transparent, and we actually deliver."

1. Position Clearly

  • We are not a freelancer, and not a giant agency. We sell quality, speed, and transparency from a tight-knit team.
  • Every pitch should emphasize team accountability and direct communication.

2. Own the Full Lifecycle

  • Every project gets design, development, deployment, and post-launch support.
  • No handoffs to external vendors. One team, one standard.

3. Target Two Tracks

Track A — Business Clients

  • Custom websites, web applications, admin panels, dashboards, ecommerce
  • Mobile apps (Flutter / React Native)
  • MVPs for startups
  • Cloud infrastructure & DevOps

Track B — Student Projects

  • Final-year projects with complete documentation, PPT, source code & deployment
  • Covers Web, Mobile, AI/ML, Cloud, IoT, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, and more
  • A reliable revenue feeder with low acquisition cost

4. Use the Process as a Sales Tool

  • The 7-step process (Discovery → Planning → Design → Dev → Testing → Deploy → Support) is not just internal ops.
  • Pitch it to clients to show we are organized, predictable, and professional.

5. Communicate Constantly

  • Regular updates, honest timelines, no surprises.
  • This is our brand — transparency.

6. Build Portfolio Aggressively

  • Every project goes live as a case study on the TechNova website.
  • A strong portfolio is our best sales tool.

7. Keep Tech Stack Modern but Stable

  • No chasing every new framework.
  • Master the stack we list: Next.js, React, Flutter, Node.js, TypeScript, Docker, AWS/Azure, PostgreSQL, MongoDB.
